Pineapple Cucumber Kale Juice

I like this this particular juice, among others, because of it's potential health benefits suchs as: Joint health and improving the body's natural detoxification properties

  • 1/4 of Pineapple
  • Handful Kale
  • 1 or 2 Cucumbers
  1. Cut the Pineapple into pieces 🍍
  2. Cut the Cucumbers into slices 🥒
  3. Put all the ingredients in the blender 🔥
  4. Enjoy! 🍹
